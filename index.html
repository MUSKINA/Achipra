About Us

The Journey of Achipra Catering

In the early '90s, Satheesn Namboothiri founded Achipra in Kottakkal, offering a warm atmosphere and amazing dishes. As demand grew, Achipra transitioned from a hotel to a catering service. Today, our team, Illa, carries on this legacy, providing top-notch catering in Kottakkal for weddings, corporate events, and parties. Achipra Catering is renowned for using fresh ingredients to create unforgettable meals, continuing Satheesn's tradition of excellence and hospitality.
